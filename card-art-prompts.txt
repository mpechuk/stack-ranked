STACK RANKED — CARD ART GENERATION PROMPTS
============================================
Generated from cards.json. One image-generation prompt per card, each preceded
by a reference line identifying the card (category, tier/subgroup, name,
cost/type, and its effect + flavor text from cards.json) so you can match
generated art back to the right entry.

MASTER STYLE (baked into every prompt below, so each block is self-contained
and can be pasted into an image model on its own):
  Retro corporate-propaganda poster art, matching the box cover illustration —
  bold black ink outlines, halftone screentone shading, worn paper-grain
  texture, dramatic radiating sunburst linework in the background, satirical
  heroic composition, screen-print poster look. Base palette of burnt orange,
  cream, and navy, lifted with a category accent color (see below). No text,
  no logos, no watermarks, no card frame — this is illustration only, the
  layout/text is added separately.

CATEGORY ACCENT COLORS (match the existing card-color coding used in the
print-and-play PDF, so art mood ties back to card type at a glance):
  Skill/Tool   -> cool steel-blue accent
  Project      -> mossy corporate-green accent
  Office Chaos -> alarm-red accent
  Training     -> muted violet accent
  Management   -> brass-gold accent
  Feedback     -> ink-purple/indigo accent (variant rule; Positive cards lifted
                  with a warm gold glow, Constructive cards cold and deflated)

FORMAT: landscape banner crop, roughly 5:2 aspect ratio (the physical card
is poker-sized, 2.5in x 3.5in, with a wide illustration band across the top —
see IMAGE_MAX_H in generate_print_and_play.py).

Each entry below has a REFERENCE line (straight from cards.json) and a PROMPT
you can paste as-is into an image-generation model.

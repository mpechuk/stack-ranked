STACK RANKED — CAREER LADDER BOARD ART GENERATION PROMPT
==========================================================
One image-generation prompt for the optional decorative header banner on the
Career Ladder board (see generate_career_ladder.py). The board's full-bleed
background reuses table-images/player-mat-background.png as-is (the same file
the Player Desk mat uses) — no new background art needed. This file covers
only the header banner that sits in the top band next to the board title.

Save the generated file as:
  table-images/career_ladder.jpeg

MASTER STYLE (same family as card-art-prompts.txt and
player-mat-art-prompts.txt, so the whole print-and-play kit — box cover,
cards, player mat, and this board — reads as one product):
  Retro corporate-propaganda poster art, matching the box cover illustration
  (see docs/CoverV2.png) — bold black ink outlines, halftone screentone
  shading, worn paper-grain texture, dramatic radiating sunburst linework,
  satirical heroic composition, screen-print poster look. Base palette of
  burnt orange, cream, and navy. No text, no logos, no watermarks — this is
  illustration only, the board's own labels/rungs are added separately by
  the PDF generator on top.

Render at high resolution (300 DPI at print size) so the PDF stays crisp:
  Header banner: ~2550 x 480 px (fills a ~5:1 landscape band)

REFERENCE: Sits to the right of the navy "STACK RANKED / CAREER LADDER
BOARD" title plate, across the top of the board. Unlike the player mat's
header (a continuation of the box cover's climbing scene), this banner should
read as the view *from the summit* — the payoff the whole ladder is racing
toward — since the board underneath lays out the full Intern-to-CEO climb as
a row of ascending platforms.

PROMPT:
Scene: A wide corner-office skyline view from the top of the org chart —
a silhouetted executive desk and a single triumphant figure in an
oversized office chair on a rooftop terrace, overlooking a sprawling city
built from stacked filing cabinets, server towers, and skyscraper-sized
binders, all bathed in a radiant sunrise. A faint corporate ladder/staircase
motif climbs up through the cloud line toward the figure, echoing the board
it sits above. Cropped as a wide horizontal banner focused on the skyline
and sunburst, with the seated figure small and silhouetted at one side
rather than the main focus (this crop needs to read well as a thin strip,
not a portrait).
Style: retro corporate-propaganda poster art (matching the box cover) —
bold black ink outlines, halftone screentone shading, worn paper grain,
radiating sunburst background, screen-print poster look. Palette: burnt
orange, cream, and navy. Format: wide landscape banner crop (~5:1), no
text/logos/watermarks.

STACK RANKED

A Game of Corporate Ascension

How to Play

🖥️ This Device Pass-and-play hot-seat and/or against AI, all on one screen. (Classic.)
📡 Host Online Run the game here; friends join from their own devices by scanning a QR. You can play too, or run board-only on a shared screen.
🔗 Join Online Connect to someone else's hosted game — as a player or a spectator.

Win Condition

🏁 Race to CEO First player promoted to CEO wins. Ends the moment someone wins the board vote. (~25–35 rounds.)
📊 The Long Game Fixed 24 rounds. Highest Final Score wins even if nobody reaches CEO.

Feedback Round

📝 Standard Feedback Each Review, everyone draws one Feedback card (±2) and may keep it or hand it to a rival. (Default.)
🔄 360° Review Each Review, everyone gets one Positive and one Constructive card, secretly gives one to another player and discards the other, then all cards flip face-up together.
